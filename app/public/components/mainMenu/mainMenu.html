About ofCourse

ofCourse is a website for students entering college or students in college who want to change their course of study. Students can view different courses, save courses to their profile and are encouraged to review courses to inform others to make decisions.

If you don't know what you want to do then don't worry about it! ofCourse has tests you can take that can recommend courses and sectors of study depending on your results. You can save all of this information to your profile and come back to it whenever. If there is someone you want to chat to about a course then our chat app will let you talk to each other.
